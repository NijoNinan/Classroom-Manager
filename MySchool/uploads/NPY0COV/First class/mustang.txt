Mustang Shelby GT500
Ford

2020

The new 2020 Shelby GT500, the most powerful street-legal Ford ever, comes with a 760-hp supercharged 5.2L V8, Tremec seven-speed dual-clutch transmission and track-developed aero, suspension and wheel technology. Massive 16.5-inch two-piece SHW rotors, six-piston fixed Brembo front calipers and advanced chassis tuning are standard. A Carbon Fiber Track Package adds carbon-fibre wheels, GT4 track wing and custom Michelin Pilot Sport Cup 2 tires. FordPass Connect, now standard, allows owners to interact with their car via a smartphone.
